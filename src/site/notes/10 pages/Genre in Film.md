**Genre in Film** is a sophomore-level film course I created for the English Department at TCU. I taught the first instance of this course in Fall 2024, with a [[10 pages/Folk horror\|Folk horror]] theme. In the future, I hope to teach the course with a [[film noir\|film noir]] theme. 

> [!INFO] Catalog description
> Studies of the aesthetic, ideological, and historical dimensions of film genre(s) such as film noir, horror, romantic comedy, the musical, etc, with examples drawn from global film industries. Topics will vary by semester and may include a comparative approach to genre.
